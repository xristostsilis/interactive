WVV Mobility: We move Würzburg

We take you to your destinations throughout Würzburg every day with modern buses and trams. Environmentally friendly and stepless low-floor vehicles, well-trained staff and extensive information and services are the strengths of WVV Mobility. For quick help on site, the mobility advisors are always available to provide you with answers about local transport.

Measures Taken

  • Strong fleet: all WVV vehicles are in use.
  • Safe entry and exit: We use cutting discs to keep contact to a minimum.
  • The highest hygiene standards: regular ventilation and cleaning of the vehicles goes without saying.
  • Mutual consideration: By all acting carefully, we can protect ourselves and others.

Drive Contactless

With your cell phone ticket in and around Würzburg

In order to make contactless ticket purchasing even better, the mobile phone ticket for Würzburg has been available since March 2021. FAIRTIQ is not only simple and safe, the app automatically determines the best daily price for your trips.

In addition, passengers who would like to travel throughout Germany can purchase the Deutschlandticket via the WVVmobil app.
